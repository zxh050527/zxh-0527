关于感情 — 爱是唯一不需要理由的事情

by 张兴昊 2026年5月

感情是唯一不需要理由的事情。

你无法计算它、衡量它、或者用逻辑去解释它。当你试图用理性去解构感情的时候，它已经悄悄溜走了。

所以，如果你感受到了什么——不要问为什么，去感受就好。

背景

从小到大，我们被教导要理性、要克制、要为未来做计划。但感情从来不在计划之中。它像一阵风，你不知道它从哪里来，也不知道它往哪里去。

这篇文字写给那些在感情里小心翼翼的人。勇敢不是不害怕，而是害怕之后仍然选择向前。

思考日记
5月3日 · 开始

今天想明白了一件事：感情不需要结果才有意义。 相遇本身就已经是礼物。

5月4日 · 对话
我：如果最后会受伤，还要开始吗？
内心：花会因为凋零就不开吗？
5月5日 · 顿悟
我：怎么样才知道这是爱？
内心：当你不再问这个问题的时候。
给谁看

写给每一个在感情里犹豫不决的人。写给那个想表达却不敢表达的你，写给那个明明在乎却假装无所谓的你。

你的心不会骗你。如果你在乎，那就承认吧。 时间有限，不要在胆怯中错过。

—— 张兴昊，关于感情

Feelings are the only things that need no reason.

You can't calculate them, measure them, or explain them with logic. The moment you try to dissect a feeling with reason, it has already slipped away.

So if you feel something — don't ask why. Just feel it.

Background

From a young age, we're taught to be rational, to restrain ourselves, to plan for the future. But feelings have never followed a plan. They're like the wind — you don't know where they come from, or where they're going.

This is for those who tiptoe around their emotions. Bravery isn't the absence of fear — it's choosing to move forward despite it.

Reflection Diary
May 3 · Beginning

Realized something today: Feelings don't need a result to be meaningful. The encounter itself is already a gift.

May 4 · Dialogue
Me: What if I get hurt in the end? Should I still start?
Inner voice: Does a flower refuse to bloom because it will wither?
May 5 · Epiphany
Me: How do I know if it's love?
Inner voice: When you stop asking that question.
Who it's for

For everyone hesitating in matters of the heart. For you who want to speak but dare not. For you who care deeply but pretend not to.

Your heart won't lie to you. If you care, own it. Time is limited. Don't let cowardice make you miss what matters.

— Xinghao Zhang, on feelings
